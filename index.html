Our Mission

Our mission is to create and maintain green spaces in urban and suburban areas, and to promote the importance of green spaces in our daily lives. We work closely with local governments, businesses, and community members to create beautiful, functional green spaces that provide a range of benefits, from improving air quality and reducing urban heat islands, to promoting physical activity and providing habitat for wildlife.

Our Methodology

Our methodology guides our process and is made up of our core values, goals, and intentions. We prioritize using sustainable materials, building green infrastructure, developing energy efficiency wherever possible, reducing water usage and waste production, improving community relationship, and providing green spaces for all.
