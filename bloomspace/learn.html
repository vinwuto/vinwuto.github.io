Assessment

Bloomspace assesses the unused corporate space and determines its potential for renovation and repurposing into a green space.

Design

We work with architects and designers to develop a plan for the green space that incorporates community input and incorporates environmentally-friendly features.

Funding

We secure funding for the project, which could come both from private investment and government grants.

Project Management

Bloomspace would oversee the entire project, managing contractors and ensuring that the project stays on schedule and within budget.

Implementation

We would oversee the physical construction of the green space, ensuring that it meets all design specifications and environmental standards.

Funding

Once the green space is complete, Bloomspace would work with the community to launch and promote the new space, ensuring that it is utilized to its fullest potential.
