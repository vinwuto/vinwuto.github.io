About Bloomspace

 

Our History


Bloomspace was founded in 2023 with the simple idea of returning unused corporate spaces back the public. As the philanthropic arm of Real Estate Corp., our funding for is secured before taking on each project. Based out of Chicago, IL, we have offices in 12 states and 19 cities.

Our Methodology


Assessment

Bloomspace assesses the unused corporate space and determine its potential for renovation and repurposing into a green space.

Design

We work with architects and designers to develop a plan for the green space that incorporates community input and incorporates environmentally-friendly features.

Funding

We will assist in creating a estimated future operating budget. We secure funding for each project through private investment, government grants, or a combination of the two. You are not responsible for initial project construction funding, but may have maintenance costs and future budgetary requirements.

Community Benefits

  • Participation from local residents
  • Respecting and supporting the ecosystem
  • Restoring biodiversity
  • Low water usage
  • Recycling waste into building materials

Project Management

Bloomspace would oversee the entire project, managing contractors and ensuring that the project stays on schedule and within budget.

Implementation

We would oversee the physical construction of the green space, ensuring that it meets all design specifications and environmental standards.

Launch

Once the green space is complete, Bloomspace would work with the community to launch and promote the new space, ensuring that it is utilized to its fullest potential.
